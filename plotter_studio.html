◆ Inspiration · Reference · Creative Lineage
Essential Resources
★ WOW
⌥github.com
Drawing With Machines
Golan Levin's CMU graduate course on pen plotter art. The most comprehensive resource in the field — covers Processing-to-SVG pipelines, vpype post-processing, axidraw workflows, offset curves, and the full creative-technical stack. The canonical reference.
Course · CMU · Golan Levin
◈plotterfiles.com
Plotter Files
Curated gallery of plotter-ready SVG files and generative sketches from the community. Dense with practical inspiration — flow fields, hatching studies, Voronoi tessellations, and more. Download and plot directly or dissect for technique.
Gallery · Community · SVG
Live Sketches & Editors
🐢turtletoy.net
Turtletoy
Browser-based sandbox for plotter-style generative art using a turtle graphics API. Thousands of community-made sketches in the open gallery — browse, fork, and remix directly in the browser. An endlessly scrollable source of algorithmic ideas.
Interactive · Turtle Graphics · Browse
⬡generated.space
Generated Space
A curated space of generative art experiments — noise-based compositions, particle systems, recursive geometries. Clean, editorial curation with an emphasis on the mathematical beauty of emergence and pattern. Good for understanding density and negative space.
Generative · Curated · Experiments
Code & Books
⌥github.com
Generative Art (erdavids)
A growing collection of Processing Java sketches built for pen plotters — clean code, well-commented, and designed with physical output in mind. Covers circle packing, flow fields, recursive subdivision and more. Good starting point for porting ideas to your own work.
Processing · Open Source · Patterns
▦generative-gestaltung.de
Generative Gestaltung
The book and companion website by Bohnacker, Groß, Laub, and Lazzeroni — the defining textbook on parametric and generative design with Processing. Hundreds of interactive examples covering grids, agents, fractals, and noise. The academic backbone of the field.
Book · Processing · Parametric Design
